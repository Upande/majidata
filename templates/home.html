About

MajiData is an initiative of the Kenyan Water Sector. The mandate to prepare MajiData was given to the Water Services Trust Fund (WSTF) by its parent ministry: the Ministry of Water and Irrigation (MWI). MajiData was funded by UN-Habitat, the German Development Bank (KfW), Google.org, GIZ and the WSTF.
